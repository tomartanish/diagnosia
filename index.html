Frequently asked questions

What is Diagnosia?

Diagnosia is a healthcare platform offering a symptom checker, doctor info, skin cancer detection, prescriptions, and posture analysis.

How accurate is the skin cancer detection?

It uses advanced image analysis for accurate results, but consult a doctor for confirmation.

How does the symptom checker work?

Input your medical history, age, and symptoms. Diagnosia analyzes this info to suggest possible diseases.

What do I need for the prescription feature?

Input your diagnosed disease to get a prescription with recommended medicines and diet charts.

How does the posture analysis work?

Our device analyzes facial expressions, body structure, and posture to detect related health issues.

Can Diagnosia help me find a doctor?

Yes, it provides a directory of doctors in India and lets you find them easily.
